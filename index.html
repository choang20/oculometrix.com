Four NHTSA Tests. One Device.

OculoMetrix digitizes the complete Standardized Field Sobriety Test battery.

Smooth Pursuit (HGN)

Tracks the subject's eye movements as they follow a moving stimulus. Measures phase correlation, amplitude ratio, and saccadic intrusion rate to detect loss of smooth pursuit.

Front Camera

Nystagmus Detection

Identifies involuntary eye jerking during head rotation. Evaluates all 6 HGN clues per NHTSA standards: smooth pursuit, max deviation, and onset prior to 45°.

Front Camera

One-Leg Stand

Monitors balance during a 30-second single-leg stance. Body-pose detection tracks sway amplitude, hops, arm raises, and foot drops with 89.5% precision.

Rear Camera

Walk & Turn

Counts heel-to-toe steps and detects 8 NHTSA clues during a 9-step tandem walk. Combined-signal step counter achieves 100% precision with 80.6% recall.

Why OculoMetrix?

Traditional field sobriety tests rely on subjective officer observation. OculoMetrix adds objective, quantitative measurement to every assessment.

  • No special hardware. Works with any iPhone X or later — the device officers already carry.
  • Court-ready data. Encrypted recordings, timestamped metrics, and signal charts provide irrefutable evidence.
  • Beyond alcohol. Eye-tracking detects impairment from cannabis, opioids, stimulants, and poly-drug use — substances breathalyzers miss entirely.
  • Validated against peer-reviewed data. Detection logic tested on PhysioNet and Health & Gait datasets with automated regression suites.
  • Binary PASS/FAIL. No ambiguous results. Every test produces a definitive determination per DUI protocol requirements.
